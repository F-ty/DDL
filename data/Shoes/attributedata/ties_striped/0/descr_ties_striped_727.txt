A haberdashery classic, hand finished in luxurious wool from Lanificio Di Tollegno (est. 1862)&mdash;one of the oldest mills in Italy. Nestled in the midst of Biella's Prealps, this mill is one of the last of its kind and is known for producing some of the finest suiting wool in the world. 2 3/4" at widest point. USA.
